About Xgamingserver

Founded in November 2020, XgamingServer (a small group of nerds) set out to create a simpler, higher performance, and more transparent game service provider. We provide all the goodies customers look for in a GSP, such as stellar performance and helpful support — yet what makes us unique is that we supply 100% dedicated resources to every game server (VDGS) and truly care about our customers experience with us. We’re not the kind of people to charge for silly extras; we provide a flat-rate service and guarantee no unwanted surprises. We’re all geeks at heart, and although we don’t have as many years of experience as other hosts, we’re dedicated to providing the best service through any means possible.
